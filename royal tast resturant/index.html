Our Story

A Legacy Seasoned
Over Two Decades

Royal Taste was born from one simple belief: exceptional food changes how people feel about a place. Since 1998, our kitchen has fused classic European technique with the bold spice traditions of South Asia — a marriage unique to our table.

Every ingredient is sourced from farmers we trust. Every plate is finished by hands that have done it thousands of times, and cared each time.

  • Farm-to-table — seasonal ingredients, sourced weekly
  • Open kitchen — nothing hidden from our guests
  • Awarded cuisine — recognised three years running
